> About me

I'm a 22-year-old web development student from Uruguay in Jóvenes a Programar 2025 edition. I'm currently learning HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

Since I was a kid, I've had a huge list of interests; programming was one of them. In school, I played with Scratch and fell in love with it. In high school I even participated in the FIRST LEGO League with my friends and my computer science teacher. I've always been interested in technology and graphic design, especially hardware and UI/UX, so I decided to start learning web development. I love learning new things; I'd say I'm "hungry to learn".

I consider myself a perfectionist, so everything needs to reach a minimum level of "perfection" to me.

I enjoy listening to music and discovering new amazing pieces; I listen to a wide range of genres, and I love to find new artists and songs. In my free time, I like to play piano, video games, and spend time procrastinating on the internet, especially on X (formerly known as Twitter).
